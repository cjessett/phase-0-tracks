** CSA MEMBER QUERIES **
============================

Display members' name, share size, eggs, and location of pick-up
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
<<-SQL
.headers on
.mode column
SELECT csa_members.name, csa_members.eggs, sizes.size, locations.location 
FROM csa_members 
JOIN sizes ON csa_members.size = sizes.id
JOIN locations ON csa_members.location = locations.id
SQL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Add member to member table
------------------------------------
<<-SQL
INSERT INTO csa_members (name, phone, eggs, size, location)
VALUES (?, ?, ?, ?, ?)
SQL
